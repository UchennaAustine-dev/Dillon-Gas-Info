Account Deletion

We understand that circumstances change. Follow the instructions below to permanently delete your Dillon Gas account and associated data.

1

Delete Through Mobile Application

The most convenient method is to use the account deletion feature built into the Dillon Gas mobile application.

  1. Open the Dillon Gas mobile application
  2. Ensure you are signed in to your account
  3. Navigate to the Profile tab in the bottom navigation
  4. Scroll to the bottom and locate the Danger Zone section
  5. Select Delete Account
  6. Enter your account password for verification
  7. Type "DELETE" in the confirmation field
  8. Confirm the permanent deletion of your account
Open Mobile App
2

Request Deletion via Email

If you cannot access the mobile application, submit a deletion request through our support email.

Email Address: support@dillongas.com

Subject Line: Account Deletion Request

Required Information:

  • Your registered email address
  • Full name associated with the account
  • Confirmation that you understand this action is irreversible
  • Reason for deletion (optional)

Important Considerations

  • Account deletion is permanent and irreversible
  • All order history, preferences, and account data will be permanently removed
  • If you have active orders, your account will be deactivated immediately and permanently deleted after order completion
  • Administrative accounts cannot be deleted through this process
  • Any unused credits or balances will be forfeited

Need Assistance?

If you encounter any difficulties during the deletion process or have questions about data handling, our support team is available to help.

Support Email: support@dillongas.com

Response Time: We typically respond within 24-48 hours and process deletion requests within 30 days as required by applicable regulations.
